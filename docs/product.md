## The Problem
1. **Finding Quality AI Talent is Hard** - The AI field is full of noise; hard to distinguish real experts from those riding the hype
2. **Fragmented Payment Models** - Different platforms, subscriptions, payment methods create friction
3. **No Unified Trust System** - No way to verify if someone actually has the skills they claim
4. **Complex Hiring** - Traditional freelancing requires contracts, invoices, negotiations

## The Solution: Vertex
A curated marketplace where:
- **AI Specialists** list their services with transparent pricing
- **Buyers** purchase services using a unified credit system
- **No Subscriptions** - Top up your wallet, pay for what you use
- **Verified Experts** - Every specialist goes through our verification process

## Core Concepts

### Credits System
- 10 credits = $1 USD
- Users top up their wallet with credits
- Credits are deducted based on service duration
- No recurring charges - pay upfront for the full duration
- Credits never expire

### Service Types
| Type | Description | Billing |
|------|-------------|---------|
| One-Time | Single deliverable (audit, setup, training) | Full payment upfront |
| Weekly | Ongoing weekly service | Full weeks paid upfront |
| Monthly | Ongoing monthly service | Full months paid upfront |
| Yearly | Long-term engagement | Full year paid upfront |

### User Roles
1. **Buyer** - Purchases AI services
2. **Specialist** - Offers AI services
3. **Admin** - Platform management

## Key Features

### For Specialists
- Create service listings with flexible pricing
- Set availability and capacity
- Receive payments directly to wallet
- Build reputation through reviews
- Verification badges for proven expertise

### For Buyers
- Browse curated AI services
- Filter by category, price, rating
- One-click purchase with credits
- Direct messaging with specialists
- Service history and receipts

### Service Categories
- **AI Strategy & Consulting** - Business transformation, roadmaps
- **Machine Learning** - Model development, training, deployment
- **Data Engineering** - Pipelines, ETL, data architecture
- **AI Integration** - API integration, automation
- **Prompt Engineering** - LLM optimization, prompt design
- **Computer Vision** - Image/video AI solutions
- **NLP & Chatbots** - Conversational AI, text analysis
- **AI Training & Workshops** - Team upskilling
- **AI Audits** - Ethics, bias, compliance review
- **Custom Development** - Bespoke AI solutions

## Revenue Model
- **Platform Fee**: 20% of transaction value
- **Specialists receive**: 80% of service price
- **Credit Purchase**: Users buy credits at face value

## Trust & Safety
- Identity verification for specialists
- Portfolio review process
- Skills assessment (optional)
- Review & rating system
- Dispute resolution
- Escrow for large projects
